What is a YouTube Thumbnail?

A YouTube thumbnail is the first visual your viewers see—it’s like a movie poster for your video. A strong thumbnail grabs attention and encourages people to click on your video.

Thumbnails can dramatically impact your video’s views, watch time, and engagement. Custom thumbnails with bold text and images perform the best.
